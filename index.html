About Me

My name is Janeth, but I go by Jay. I am a bilingual (Spanish and English) associate marriage and family therapist. I am passionate about helping individuals cope with life challenges and work collaboratively towards their self-defined goals.

I prioritize building a deep therapeutic relationship with my clients. My therapeutic approach is multi-faceted and unique to each individual. I provide a safe space for individuals to be able to work on their challenges with no judgement, compassion, creativity and collaboration.

I'm grateful you're here and reaching out for support. This is the first step towards growth and well-being.

Treatment Approach

ABA

Applied Behavior Analysis focuses on improving specific behaviors through reinforcement strategies.

CBT

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy helps identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors.

CPT

Cognitive Processing Therapy is effective for treating PTSD by challenging unhelpful beliefs.

Family Systems

Views problems as patterns or systems that need adjusting rather than residing in an individual.

Mindfulness-Based (MBCT)

Combines cognitive therapy with meditative practices and mindfulness techniques.

Motivational Interviewing

Helps clients find the motivation to make positive behavioral changes.

Person-Centered

Emphasizes unconditional positive regard and support to help clients make changes.

Psychoanalytic

Explores unconscious patterns of thought and behavior that developed from childhood.

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT)

Focuses on solutions rather than problems, emphasizing clients' strengths.

Strength-Based

Focuses on the individual's strengths and resilience rather than weaknesses.

Structural Family Therapy

Examines and addresses patterns of interaction within families to create healthier dynamics.
